Our mission.

As the world’s number 1 job site, our mission is to help people get jobs. We need talented, passionate people working together to make this happen. We are looking to grow our teams with people who share our energy and enthusiasm for creating the best experience for job seekers.

The team.

We are a rapidly growing and highly-capable Engineering organization building the most popular job site on the planet. Every month, over 250 million people count on Indeed to help them find jobs, publish their resumes, process their job applications, and connect employers to qualified candidates for their job openings. With engineering hubs in Seattle, San Francisco, Austin, Tokyo, Singapore, Hyderabad and Vancouver, we are improving people's lives all around the world, one job search at a time.

Singapore Base Salary Range: 115,000 - 142,000 SGD per year

Your job.

If you are an engineer who's passionate about building innovative products that scale to tens of millions of pageviews a day, we are looking for you. Indeed offers highly determined engineers like you a multi-faceted development ecosystem with short release cycles. Every week sees the new release of multiple products that meet the growing needs of millions of jobseekers worldwide.

Indeed’s International Business Unit (IBU) is hiring full-stack software engineers in Singapore in two of our International Platform teams: International Payments and Challenge Markets EU (European Union).

International Payments Team

The current payment system in Indeed is a complex suite of microservices that is responsible for millions of dollars of transactions daily. The International Payments team, together with our partners in Austin are responsible for improving its architecture so as to enable us to scale internationally.

Challenge Markets EU Team

Our Challenge Markets EU Team is focused on bringing the best possible Indeed experience tailored to job seekers and employers in the European Union. As part of this team, you will work on various projects to help connect job seekers with employers in European Union countries.

As a full-stack software engineer, you’ll be a part of a small, dynamic, and highly committed team. You will collaborate with other engineers, product managers, UX designers, and data scientists, working on building and maintaining services, as well as developing state-of-the-art solutions powering our payments platforms. What you do will have a real impact on real people.

At Indeed, we love data. As a part of a highly data-driven organization, you won’t have to guess at the impact you’re having; you’ll leverage our large-scale data analysis infrastructure to define, measure, and test your hypotheses and deliver the best possible experience for the jobseeker. Our primary technology stack is Java-based, but we believe strongly in using the right tool for the right job. As such, we’re employing best-of-breed technologies and incorporating them into our stack. This includes things like Spring Boot, Node.JS, Next.JS, React/Redux, MongoDB, Mesos, Kubernetes, to name a few. Additionally, sometimes the right tool for the job doesn’t exist, so we’ve created and open-sourced a number of our core systems. You can explore them on Github and YouTube.

About you.

Required Skills

  • 5+ years of experience planning, architecting, designing and developing platform software

  • Proven track record in delivering and maintaining scalable solutions in the payments processing domain

  • Proficiency developing object-oriented software in Java and related frameworks (or equivalent), especially in concurrent and async usages

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to present complex technical information in a clear and concise manner to a variety of audiences

  • BS in Computer Science or related area, or relevant work experience

Desired Skills

  • Strong knowledge of Java, Spring Framework, Protobuf, ReST, GraphQL, and JSON.

  • Strong knowledge of one or more SQL and/or NoSQL databases

  • Experience with design and development of scalable web services and platforms

  • Experience with service-oriented and event-driven system architectures

  • Strong knowledge of one or more cloud-based environments (e.g. AWS, Google Cloud, Azure)

  • Solid knowledge of data structures and algorithms

  • Strong problem-solving skills and a passion for debugging complex issues

  • Proficiency with Linux based environments

  • Experience with git, Continuous Integration systems (Hudson/Jenkins) & Bug Tracking (Jira) tools

  • Experience with using agile methodologies for software development

  • Experience with Test-Driven Development
